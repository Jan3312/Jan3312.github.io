Death

Kobi passed away on January 27, 2020, from Spleen cancer. She was always so happy to see us, greet us, or beg us for a snack/food with her big brown eyes whenever she had the chance. She always shined bright in the light like a crazy diamond. Whether it was a sign or by mere chance that she passed away the day after Kobe Bryant her death too was also sudden. Rest in peace my beloved puppy.
