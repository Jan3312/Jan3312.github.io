About Miniature Schnauzers

Miniature Schnauzers are joyful little dogs, that originate from Germany, and were first bred in the late 19th century. Miniature Schnauzers are a breed of Terrier and are a mixing of Affenpinschers with small Standard Schnauzers. These dogs can range anywhere from 11 - 20 pounds and can live up to 12 - 15 years of age.
